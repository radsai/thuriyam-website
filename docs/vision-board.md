# Vision Board: Website Revamp

## 1. Strategic Narrative Shift

**Move away from:**
- Developer-focused copy: "Build Production-Ready Agents in Minutes", "Simple to Build, Production-Ready by Default"
- CTAs like "Ready to Build Your First Agent? Start building in minutes. No credit card required."
- Feature-level phrases: "No Technical Skills Required", "Instant Updates", "Talk to your agent"

**Move toward:**
- Business-outcome narrative
- Audience: business leaders, ops managers, process owners (not developers)

---

## 2. Five Pillars (Target Narrative)

| Pillar | Concept | Messaging |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Proactive Agents** | Anticipate, initiate, act without waiting for prompts | "Agents that act before you ask" / "From reactive to proactive" |
| **Outcome-Driven** | Success = business outcomes (revenue, SLA, compliance) | "Define the outcome. Agents deliver." / "Outcomes, not outputs" |
| **Event-Driven** | Triggered by events, not manual invocation | "When it happens, agents respond" / "Event-triggered autonomy" |
| **Less Manual / Reduced HITL** | HITL only where judgment is needed | "Humans where judgment matters. Agents everywhere else." |
| **RPA Sunset, Agentic Rise** | RPA = brittle; Agentic = adaptive | "Beyond RPA: From scripts to intelligence" |

---

## 3. Phrases to Avoid

- "Simple to Build, Production-Ready by Default"
- "Build Production-Ready Agents in Minutes"
- "Ready to Build Your First Agent? Start building in minutes. No credit card required."
- "No coding required"
- "No credit card required"
- "Talk to your agent"

---

## 4. Hero & Section Themes

**Hero:**
- "Proactive Agents That Deliver Outcomes" or "The Agentic Platform for Autonomous Operations"
- Subline: Focus on outcomes (less manual work, automate decisions, respond to events)

**Section themes:**
- Proactive by Default
- Outcome-First Design
- Event-Driven Architecture
- Beyond RPA
- HITL Where It Matters

---

## 5. Principle: Narrative Blended on Landing Page

Events, outcomes, and "why agentic" are **not** separate pages. They are narrative threads woven seamlessly into the landing page. All concepts combine in one flow.
